# my

Cards in the user's list.

**Canonical:** `my`  
**Aliases:** —  
**Domain:** Card (list membership; not a per-face attribute)

## Operators

| Operator | Behavior | Example |
|----------|----------|---------|
| `:` | In list | `q=my:list` |
| `=` | In list | `q=my:trash` |

## Allowed values

- **`list`:** Cards in your list (My List). Matches cards you've added to your list.
- **`trash`:** Cards in your trash. Matches cards you've removed from your list.

## Notes

- `q=my:list` filters to cards in the user's My List. Use with [my:list](?doc=reference/special/my-list) for semantics.
- Whether matches are deduplicated by oracle or by printing depends on list contents (names vs specific printings).
