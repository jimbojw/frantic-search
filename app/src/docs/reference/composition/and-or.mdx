# AND and OR

## Implicit AND

By default, multiple terms are combined with AND. A card must match **all** terms to appear in the results.

| Query | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| `t:creature c:green` | Creatures that are green |
| `f:modern pow>=4` | Modern-legal cards with power 4 or greater |

## Explicit OR

Use the keyword `OR` (case-insensitive) to match cards that satisfy **either** term.

| Query | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| `c:red OR c:blue` | Red or blue cards |
| `t:instant OR t:sorcery` | Instants or sorceries |

## Parentheses

Use parentheses to group sub-expressions and control precedence.

| Query | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| `(c:red OR c:blue) t:creature` | Red or blue creatures |
| `(t:creature OR t:artifact) f:commander` | Creatures or artifacts legal in Commander |

## Exact name

Use `!"..."` to match the full card name exactly (no substring). This is useful for disambiguating cards with similar names.

| Query | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| `!"Lightning Bolt"` | Exactly "Lightning Bolt" |
| `!"Lightning Bolt" f:vintage` | Lightning Bolt in Vintage |

Exact-name terms participate in AND/OR composition like any other term.
